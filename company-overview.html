Company Overview

Fleethub Rwanda is a proudly Rwandan company on a mission to become the country’s leading one-stop hub for car rental services.

Whether you're a private car owner or a car rental company with an idle vehicle, Fleethub Rwanda offers the perfect solution—transforming unused vehicles into income-generating assets. We connect individual car owners and rental businesses across Rwanda, creating a unified, efficient car rental network.

Seamless Booking on Any Device

Our powerful Booking Engine makes renting a car simple and intuitive. Customers can check real-time vehicle availability, compare options, request quotes, and make reservations—all from their smartphones or tablets. Every booking is automatically synced with our platform, ensuring that schedules are always up-to-date.

E-Signature Convenience

Thanks to our Digital Signature feature, contracts and quotes can be signed securely and conveniently on any mobile device, eliminating paperwork and speeding up the rental process.

Empowering Our Partners

With our exclusive App Partner platform, rental partners can manage bookings from any device—PC, tablet, or smartphone. Access valuable insights with detailed statistics on bookings and services year after year. It’s a powerful tool designed to enhance partnerships and drive growth across the industry.
