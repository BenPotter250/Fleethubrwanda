Rental Requirements

To become a verified rental partner, your company must meet the following:

  • Valid RDB business registration certificate.
  • Valid RURA license for car rental services.
  • Vehicle ownership and registration documents.
  • Valid insurance documents (minimum: third-party).
  • National ID or passport of the company representative.
  • Signed FleetHub Partner Agreement.
